Ansher Capital arranged successful dept financing for Alif Moliya

10.09.2024

Ansher Capital is pleased to announce that it has successfully arranged debt financing for Alif Uzbekistan from the International Islamic Trade Finance Corporation (ITFC). This is Ansher Capital’s first debt financing deal of 2025 for companies from Uzbekistan, secured from ITFC, a member of the Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) group, through Ipak Yuli Bank in collaboration with TMT Global Advisers (UK).  

About Alif Moliya: Alif Moliya is one of the leading fintech companies in Central Asia, with operations in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and Pakistan. Known for its commitment to Islamic finance principles, Alif Moliya offers a super-app that integrates services like e-payments, transfers, public transport payments, e-commerce, and its flagship BNPL-service “Alif Nasiya”.  

About ITFC:The International Islamic Trade Finance Corporation (ITFC), a member of the Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) group, provides financing to support trade in OIC (Organization of Islamic Cooperation) member countries and fosters economic development through Islamic financial instruments.  

About Ipak Yuli Bank:Ipak Yuli Bank is one of the largest private commercial banks in Uzbekistan, offering a wide range of financial services for individuals and businesses. The bank actively supports the development of small and medium-sized enterprises by introducing modern technologies and innovative solutions in the banking sector.  

About Ansher Capital:Ansher Capital is a leading investment bank in Uzbekistan, a subsidiary of Ansher Investments LLP (UK). It specializes in attracting a wide range of debt and equity financing for companies and banks in Central Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. Ansher Capital also focuses on M&A transactions in the financial sector, pharmaceuticals, and renewable energy. Ansher Capital is proud of arranging debt financing for Alif Moliya and wishes continued success.
