ABOUT US

25 YEARS OF PERFECTION

Ansher Investments LLP is a London-based holding company that conducts its business in Uzbekistan through its subsidiary, Ansher Capital LLC , which is the largest investment bank in Uzbekistan by capitalization and the most experienced in this market.

Ansher Capital LLC is the first traditional investment-banking boutique firm in Uzbekistan, which specializes in attracting a wide range of debt and equity financing for companies and banks in Central Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. Ansher Capital also focuses on M&A transactions in the financial sector, pharmaceuticals, and renewable energy.

Our Mission

Аnsher Capital is dedicated to delivering innovative investment banking solutions that drive sustainable growth and economic progress. We aim to empower our clients and partners through strategic insights, expert guidance, and a commitment to excellence, fostering long-term value for Uzbekistan and the global markets we serve.

Over its period of operation, Ansher Capital has successfully attracted various types of foreign investments to a number of enterprises and banks of Uzbekistan. Thus, Ansher Capital has paved the way for international investors to invest directly in the region while providing them with top quality guidance and feedback from on-site specialists and analysts. The company has curved its competitive edge through provision of high quality package services, which enables the clients to enjoy a "one window stop” approach
