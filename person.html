Board of Directors

Abboskhon Rasulev

Member of the Board of Directors of Ansher Investments LLP, CEO of Ansher Capital LLC

Abboskhon Rasulev

Mr. Rasulev is serving as the Chief Executive Officer of Ansher Capital LLC, an Investment Banking arm of Ansher Investments LLP since 2023. Before his position as CEO, he was the Senior Managing Director (TPM) (2022 - 2023), Managing Director (Trading Operations) (2020 - 2022), and Managing Director for consultancy services (2020) in Ansher Capital LLC.

Mr. Rasulev also served as Head of the Representative Office of Ansher Investments LLP in Uzbekistan from 2017-2023.

Mr Rasulev started his career as Deputy General Director in Movarounnaxr Invest LLC, an investment company for equities in 2016, and became General Director in 2017 until 2020.

He holds a B.A. (Hons) degree from Westminster International University in Tashkent.

His mother tongue is the Uzbek language. Mr. Rasulev is fluent in Russian and English.
