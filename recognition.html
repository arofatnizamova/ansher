Recognition

National Association of Investment Institutions - 2024

In 2024, Anvar Rasulev, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Ansher Investments LLP, was awarded for a significant contribution to the development of the sector by the National Association of Investment Institutions. This honor recognizes Anvar Rasulev’s exceptional efforts and leadership in advancing the investment market in Uzbekistan

National Association of Investment Institutions - 2023

In March 2023, Ansher Capital was once again awarded the "Best Reliable Manager of Investment Assets" title by the National Association of Investment Institutions, based on 2022 results. This continued recognition underscores the company’s excellence in asset management and its vital role in Uzbekistan’s investment market

National Association of Investment Institutions - 2022

In 2022, Ansher Capital was awarded the title of "Best Reliable Manager of Investment Assets" by the National Association of Investment Institutions. This recognition, based on the results of 2021, celebrates the company’s commitment to excellence and its pivotal role in managing investment assets in Uzbekistan

EmeaFinance CEE & CIS Bank Awards 2008

Ansher Capital was awarded the Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan at the EmeaFinance CEE & CIS Bank Awards 2008. This honor acknowledges the company’s strong performance and leadership in the region’s financial markets, reinforcing its competitive edge and industry expertise

Euromoney - Award for Excellence - 2008

In 2008, Ansher Capital was once again recognized by Euromoney as the Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan. This award emphasizes the company’s long-standing excellence and dominance in the country’s investment banking industry, further solidifying its status as a market leader

The Centre for Securities Market - 2006

This certificate reaffirms Ansher Capital’s role as the Best Investment Advisor of 2006, awarded by The Centre for Securities Market. The repeated recognition highlights the company’s consistent performance and strong leadership in the financial advisory sector

Euromoney - Award for Excellence - 2006

Euromoney honored Ansher Capital with the Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan award for the third consecutive year in 2006. This accolade is a testament to the company's enduring excellence and its position as a leading financial institution in the region

Centre for Coordination and Control of the Securities Market - 2006

In recognition of Ansher Capital’s exceptional investment advisory services, the Centre for Coordination and Control of the Securities Market awarded the company the Best Investment Advisor of the Year 2006, acknowledging its outstanding role in shaping Uzbekistan’s investment landscape

Euromoney - Award for Excellence - 2005

For the second consecutive year, Ansher Capital was named Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan by Euromoney in 2005. This award highlights the company's continued excellence in the investment banking industry, showcasing its sustained leadership and market impact

Centre for Coordination and Control of the Securities Market - 2005

In 2005, Ansher Capital received the prestigious award for Best Investment Advisor in Uzbekistan from the Centre for Coordination and Control of the Securities Market under the State Committee for State Property Affairs. This recognition celebrates the company’s exemplary investment consulting services and its exceptional performance in the local market

The Centre for Securities Market - 2004

Ansher Capital is honored with the Best Investment Advisor of the Year award for 2004, presented by The Centre for Securities Market. This recognition underscores the company’s expertise, commitment, and significant contributions to the investment advisory field, cementing its reputation as a top advisor in Uzbekistan

Euromoney - Award for Excellence - 2004

This certificate recognizes Ansher Capital for its outstanding performance in the financial industry, awarded the prestigious title of Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan in 2004. This accolade reflects the company’s leadership and excellence in the investment banking sector, marking a significant achievement in its continued success
